PrepRadii App

PrepRadii is a peer-learning platform that connects students nearby based on their exam goals, enabling instant chat and collaborative learning.

  • 1 Connect & Learn – Discover nearby learners based on location, interests, and exam goals.
  • 2 Instant Chat – Communicate in real time to discuss topics, share resources, and clear doubts instantly.
  • 3 Collaborate & Grow – Create or join focused study groups to learn together and stay motivated.
